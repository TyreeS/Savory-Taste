Our Story

Savory Taste, a story mixed with a love for cooking and friendship. Founders, Samantha, Tyree & Aaliah started small cooking business to start sharing their wonderful recipes with the world. After working in the food industry for over 8-10 years, we wanted to create something that would allow us to share our experiences with the world and just have fun together. We hope you enjoy this as much as we do.
